ERC Project

CONSENT

ERC Consolidator Grant 2024

Why do electoral losers accept being governed by the winners? This project investigates the motivations behind losers' consent — and what happens when it breaks down in established democracies. We combine surveys, experimental methods, text analyses and qualitative techniques to study one of the foundational pillars of democratic governance.
